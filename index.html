About Your Massage

The Swedish Massage is a full body massage, consisting of five main strokes:
effleurage, petrissage, tapotement, friction, and vibration.

The strokes are administered in a long, flowing style, and target major muscle groups.
The result is complete, pure relaxation.

Swedish massage also has health benefits.
Besides reducing stress levels,massage also improves circulation,
helps to reduce the buildup of harmful toxins, and may even reduce the appearance of stretch marks.
